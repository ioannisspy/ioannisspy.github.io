## [Chat-SEC](https://chat-sec.com/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}

Fast and accurate, AI-powered financial document analysis for teaching and research.

Chat-SEC lets students and faculty ask targeted questions about specific segments of firms' SEC filings. It retrieves exact filings by ticker, date, and form type, extracts relevant sections such as Risk Factors, MD&A, and Financial Statements, and grounds responses in verified filing text to support accurate analysis of long financial documents.

## [Money-Stuff Insights](https://money-stuff.cc/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}

Private, local-first app for exploring Matt Levine's *Money Stuff* newsletter.

Money-Stuff Insights lets you ask questions over a personal archive of Money Stuff posts. It surfaces interesting stories, key facts, and references from Matt Levine's writing, returns concise summaries with the dates he covered each topic, and links to the original text for full, verifiable context.
